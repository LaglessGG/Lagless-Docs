# Lagless Modpack Installer 

<Callout type="warn" title="Back Up Your Files">
    Before installing a new modpack, back up important data.
    Installing a modpack may overwrite or delete existing files.
</Callout>

<Steps>
    <Step>
	Head over to our [lagless panel](https://panel.lagless.gg/) and log in. 
    </Step>
    <Step>
	On the left menu, look for **Modpacks** 
    </Step>
    <Step>
	In this example, we're going to look for **BMC4** which is hosted with Modrinth.  
	Depending on your modpack, you may need to switch provider.
	![Searching Modpack](/images/minecraft/mods/modpacksearch.png)
    </Step>
    <Step>
	Once you have found the modpack you want to install, press the install button. 
    </Step>
    <Step>
	Select your version, and whether you want to **Delete files** or not. Keep in mind, this is irreversible. So make backups if you have not already.
	![Install Modpack](/images/minecraft/mods/installmodpack.png)
    </Step>
    <Step>
	Press **Install modpack**, it may take a few moments to install.
    </Step>
    <Step>
	Enjoy the new modpack.
    </Step>
</Steps>

# Uploading a Modpack

If you have a modpack that isn't available on any of our providers or are using a custom modpack to play with friends, you'll need to upload it via SFTP.

<Card href="/using-the-panel/sftp" title="SFTP Clients">
  Read our existing guide on how to connect and upload your server with SFTP.
</Card>

After uploading your zipped modpack, navigate to the <b>File Manager</b>, click on the uploaded file, and select <b>Unarchive</b>.

![unzip](/images/minecraft/unzip-world.png)
